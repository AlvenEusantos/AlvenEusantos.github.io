About Me

Hi! I am an electrical engineering graduate from San Diego State University. I am actively looking for opportunities where I can
showcase and build on the knowledge and experience I earned through my education and various projects. I have working knowledge in
RF engineering, embedded systems programming, and mixed signal circuit design. My current interests lie in RF engineering,
but I am always willing to increase my breadth of knowledge and experience!


Highlighted Experience


WETnet

Created a real-time weather update application for San Diego State University.


Microwave Component Design

Designed and tested various RF components using AWR Microwave Office.


Digital Circuit Design

Utilized Cadence Virtuoso to design, test, and layout transistor level logic gates.


Temperature Controlled Fan

Designed a controller that would increase a fan's speed with an increase of temperature.


Analog Circuit Design

Designed, tested, and built various analog circuits using SPICE software and lab equipment.


PASE

Groundbreaker and president for the revival of SDSU's Pacific Asian Society of Engineers.


Engineering Olympics

Program Co-coordinator for SDSU's first Engineering Olympics which hosted over 60 participants.


PACES

Integrated the Peer Advising Center for Engineering Students into SDSU's College of Engineering.


Project Lead the Way

Worked as an intern to solve technical problems for over 200 participants of summer training.
